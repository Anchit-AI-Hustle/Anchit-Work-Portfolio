How it works
One closed loop — the same D2C lifecycle model, whether the "product" is a consumer brand or a résumé. By default it runs on anchit-tandon.com; point it at any brand and it generalises.
01
Benchmark

Read the subject's category and comparable set — for me, the senior product/growth market and its response-rate bands.

02
Segment

Build the audiences — for a brand, RFM buyers; for a candidate, the employer types most likely to convert.

03
Plan

Design the funnel: stages, a week-by-week calendar and the campaign for each moment.

04
Generate

Write the outreach — on-brand mailers, or recruiter emails in the candidate's own voice.

05
Automate

Trigger the right follow-up at the right moment — cart-abandon, or interview thank-you.

06
Measure

KPIs close the loop — repeat-rate, or interviews booked and offers landed.
